## What Was Done

### 1. CRITICAL: Fixed Live/Backtest Scoring Mismatch
- **DaytradeMode._scoreSignal()** was applying `ictWeight`/`fpWeight` TWICE:
  - Once at signal creation (`sig.confidence * ictWeight`)
  - Again implicitly via threshold comparison against weight-normalized values
- Backtest was compensating by dividing by weight (`sig.confidence / ictWeight`)
- **Result**: Live signals scored ~65% LOWER than backtest — almost nothing passed thresholds in live
- **Fix**: Removed weight multiplication from both live `_scoreSignal()` and backtest `_evaluateSignal()`
- Both now use RAW confidence values on the same scale

### 2. CRITICAL: Fixed Live Bot Position Sizing (Per-Asset Risk)
- **BinanceLiveBotRunner** and **LiveBotRunner** used `config.risk[regime].riskPercent` flat for ALL assets
- Did NOT apply per-asset `riskMultiplier` from assetProfiles.js
- **Result**: SOL live risked 0.5% instead of 0.4% (25% too much), BTC risked 0.5% instead of 0.5% (ok by accident)
- **Fix**: Both runners now multiply `baseRiskPercent * profile.riskMultiplier`
- Paper bot (BotRunner) already had this correct via `_mergeConfig`

## Revised Analysis — What's Actually Wrong

### The Real Problem: Signal Quality, Not Stop Width
The system has TWO independent problems:
1. **Too many weak signals pass** — raw confidence values from Precompute.js produce scores that easily reach thresholds
2. **Stop losses are noise traps** — even at 2.0 ATR, 97% of SL exits are losses

These are connected: weak signals enter at bad prices, then get stopped out before trailing can activate.

### The Real Edge
- **Trailing stops**: 78% WR, the money maker
- **Partial TP**: 100% WR, locks in profits
- These ONLY work when the initial entry is good enough to reach trailing activation (0.9-1.2 ATR profit)

### What Needs To Happen
1. **Much higher signal thresholds** — only allow the STRONGEST signals through
   - ETH: minConfluenceScore → 0.80, minSoloScore → 0.90
   - SOL: minConfluenceScore → 0.75, minSoloScore → 0.85
   - BTC: minConfluenceScore → 0.78, minSoloScore → 0.88
2. **Kill breakeven** — it's scratching trades at 0% WR. Remove or set activation to 1.5 ATR (above trailing activation)
3. **OR: Remove regular SL entirely** — rely only on trailing + partial TP
   - Risk: a few trades might blow up
   - Benefit: eliminate the -$16K noise trap
