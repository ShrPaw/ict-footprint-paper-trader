## KEY FINDINGS

### The Previous Analysis Was Wrong

The original conclusion ("no entry edge") was based on 66 trades — statistically
insufficient. The expanded dataset (496,881 signals) reveals:

### 1. ENTRY HAS NO PREDICTIVE POWER (Confirmed at Scale)

- RR 1:2 fixed exit: 51.5% WR, $2.4M total — slightly above breakeven
- Random exit baseline: 47.8% WR, $3.2M total — OUTPERFORMS directional signals
- Directional signals LOSE $783K compared to random coin flip
- Conclusion: current features do not capture what moves price

### 2. DURATION IS THE ONLY REAL EDGE

- Trades ≤1h: 100% WR, +$6.8M
- Trades ≤4h: 100% WR, +$18.7M
- Trades 24-48h: 73% WR, -$5.1M
- Trades >48h: 57% WR, -$25.9M
- Every trade that closes quickly wins. Every stale trade dies.

### 3. TRAILING STOPS LOSE MONEY (Unfiltered)

- With unrestricted signals, trailing stops produce PF 0.92, -$4.2M
- The production system's PF 2.02 was survivorship bias from 99.94% filtering
- Trailing stops only "work" when you filter out 99.94% of opportunities

### 4. FEATURE INTERACTIONS THAT WORK

- ABSORPTION regime: PF 1.53-3.87 (but tiny sample)
- TRENDING_DOWN + short: PF 1.14, 83K trades
- LOW_VOL + short: PF 1.09, 65K trades
- TRENDING_DOWN|NV|ME: PF 5.64, 429 trades
- TRENDING_DOWN|NV|LE: PF 5.35, 317 trades

### 5. TEMPORAL VALIDATION

- 2022: +$1.07M (crypto winter — favorable)
- 2023: +$961K (still favorable)
- 2024: -$1.79M (regime shift)
- 2025: -$4.34M (catastrophic)
- System has NO temporal consistency across years

### 6. EMERGENCY STOPS

- 55,890 emergency exits across all signals
- Total emergency loss: -$48.4M
- Average duration: 4,593 minutes (3.2 days)
- Emergency stops are stale trades, not bad entries
