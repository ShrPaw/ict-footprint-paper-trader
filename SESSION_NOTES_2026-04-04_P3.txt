# SESSION NOTES — 2026-04-04 (Session #16 — Phase 2.7 through 5.5)

## What Was Done

### PHASE 2.7: EDGE EXTRACTABILITY (6-test analysis)

Built `analysis/edge-extractability-engine.js`. Ran all 5 candidate edges through friction sensitivity, timing flexibility, holding period optimization, regime dependency, capital efficiency, and risk structure tests.

**Result:** All 5 classified as ROBUST & EXTRACTABLE initially, but every single one flagged for unbounded tail risk (worst 1% MAE: 11-31%). Revised classification:
- BTC Displacement: REAL BUT FRAGILE
- ETH Displacement: REAL BUT FRAGILE  
- SOL Higher-Low: REAL BUT FRAGILE
- SOL Stop-Run: STATISTICAL NOT TRADEABLE
- XRP Vol Surge: STATISTICAL NOT TRADEABLE

### PHASE 3: EXECUTION VALIDATION (Risk Overlay)

Built `analysis/phase3-risk-overlay.js`. Ran the 3 fragile edges through a FIXED risk overlay (stop 2×ATR, trail activate 1×ATR, trail distance 1.5×ATR, max hold 24h).

**Result:** ALL 3 EDGES DESTROYED. PF 0.64-0.73. The hard stop (2×ATR) has 0% win rate across all edges. 21-25% of trades hit the hard stop before trailing could activate, wiping out more than the trailing system generated.

Key finding: Tail risk IS controlled (85-92% reduction) but the cost of protection exceeds the edge's margin.

### PHASE 4: DURATION EDGE DISCOVERY

Built `analysis/phase4-duration-edge.js`. Tested whether TIME (not ENTRY) is the real edge. 5 experiments: random entry baseline, conditional time edge, path analysis, entry irrelevance test, time-based exit system.

**Result:** NO universal duration edge. ETH and SOL are pure noise at every duration. BUT:
- TRENDING_UP regime has strong positive bias (ETH: t=6.41 at 24h, SOL: t=2.83, BTC: t=2.95)
- Signal-based entries are 5-47× better than random (real information)
- 77% of "losing" trades went positive at some point
- Pure time-based exits fail after fees

### PHASE 4.5: REGIME DETECTION VALIDATION

Built `analysis/phase4.5-regime-detection.js`. 5 independent detectors tested against ground truth (24h forward return ≥ 1%).

**Result:** NO ROBUST DETECTOR EXISTS. All detectors have lift ≈ 1.0 (no better than random). The regime edge exists but cannot be detected in real-time. By the time indicators confirm the trend, 60-70% of the move is consumed.

### PHASE 5: INFORMATION EDGE DISCOVERY (Funding Rates)

Built `analysis/phase5-funding-edge.js`. Tested 5 hypotheses using Binance funding rate data (8h intervals, 4,600+ events per asset).

**Result:** 33 statistically significant findings. Extreme funding (both high AND low) predicts positive forward returns. Cumulative funding drain predicts positive returns. Negative funding streaks predict positive returns.

### PHASE 5.5: STRUCTURAL EDGE VALIDATION

Built `analysis/phase5.5-funding-validation.js`. 7-test validation of top funding signals.

**Result:** 5 out of 7 signals classified as ROBUST STRUCTURAL EDGE:

1. BTC Extreme Low Funding (p10): 8/8 score, 466 events, +0.58% at 48h, t=3.07, positive in ALL years and ALL regimes
2. BTC Extreme High Funding (p95): 7/8, 233 events, +0.65% at 48h, t=2.32
3. BTC High Cumulative Drain: 7/8, 897 events, +0.34% at 48h, t=2.87
4. ETH High Cumulative Drain: 8/8, 466 events, +0.55% at 48h, t=2.76, positive ALL years/ALL regimes
5. XRP High Cumulative Drain: 7/8, 466 events, **+1.81% at 48h, t=4.17** (strongest signal)
6. XRP Extreme Low Funding (p10): 8/8, 466 events, +1.04% at 48h, t=3.56

**Key differences from OHLCV edges:**
- Survives friction (mean 3-13× above fees vs 2-4× for OHLCV)
- Survives 8h entry delay (60-97% retention vs 37-56%)
- Regime independent (83-100% of regimes positive)
- Year stable (67-100% of years positive)
- Structural mechanism (funding forces position closing = causal, not correlative)

**One remaining flag:** Unbounded tail risk (worst 1% MAE: 12-27%). BUT the edge has sufficient margin to survive a properly-sized risk overlay, unlike OHLCV edges.
